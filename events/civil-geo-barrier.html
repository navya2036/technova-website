Geo Barrier

Team Size: 3

Event Description

A Geo barrier is a structure designed to provide stability and support to slopes or embankments. It is commonly used in civil engineering and geotechnical applications to prevent soil erosion, control landslides, and create retaining walls.

Geo barrier are typically constructed using a combination of geo synthetic materials and natural or engineered fill materials. The geo synthetic materials, such as geo textiles and geo grids, provide reinforcement and confinement to the soil, while the fill materials add mass and stability to the structure.

Rules

Structural Stability: Evaluation of the Geo barrier ability to withstand loads and resist deformation.

Aesthetic Appeal: Assessment of the wall's visual attractiveness and integration with the surroundings.

Construction Quality: Review of the construction techniques employed and the overall workmanship.

Innovation: Recognition of unique and creative design concepts or engineering solutions.
