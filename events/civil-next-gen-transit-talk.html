Next-Gen Transit Talk

Team Size: 1

Event Description

Next-Gen Transit Talk is a technical poster-based innovation and presentation challenge conducted as part of Technova 2026. The event encourages students to explore emerging, disruptive, and future-oriented concepts in Transportation Engineering and present them effectively to a jury panel and audience. Participants are required to design a technical poster on a chosen emerging transportation topic and defend their ideas through an oral presentation, demonstrating technical understanding, innovation, and real world applicability.

Rules

Posters must be original; plagiarism will result in disqualification

All technical claims should be conceptually justified

Data, figures, and images must be properly referenced

Teams must adhere strictly to time limits

Jury's decision will be final and binding
