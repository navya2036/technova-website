Event Description

Hardware Debugging is the ultimate test for electronics enthusiasts! Participants are presented with faulty electronic circuits and must use their analytical skills and technical knowledge to identify and fix the problems.

This competition evaluates your circuit analysis, troubleshooting methodology, and hands-on debugging skills. You'll work with real hardware setups that contain deliberately introduced faults.
