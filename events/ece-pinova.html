Event Description

The workshop is designed to introduce students to practical AI applications in Signal Processing and Edge Computing.

Participants gain hands-on exposure to building real-world AI-based signal processing solutions.

Workshop Details

First half of the workshop:

  • Designing and training AI models for signal processing tasks
  • Data preparation and preprocessing
  • Feature extraction techniques
  • AI model development and validation

Second half of the workshop:

  • Deployment of trained AI models on Raspberry Pi
  • Real-time edge inference
  • Demonstration of complete AI-to-hardware workflows
