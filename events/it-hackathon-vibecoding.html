Event Description

Hackathon / Vibecoding is a national-level coding competition for B.Tech students across India, where teams tackle real-world challenges in a timed format. It emphasizes innovation, problem-solving, clean code, and collaboration through team-based projects judged on usability, originality, and presentation.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team should consist of maximum 4 members.
  • Arrive 30 minutes before start time; event begins promptly with problem statement reveal.
  • Evaluation will be done on innovation, usefulness, originality, social impact, and presentation.
