She INNOVATES: Deep Tech Pitch Challenge

Team Size: 3

Event Description

SheInnovates is a startup-focused idea-pitching contest aimed at empowering women students to transform bold technical ideas into future-ready ventures.

The event provides a platform for participants to pitch technology-driven business concepts that address real-world challenges.

Emphasis is placed on innovation, scalability, and market relevance of the proposed ideas.

The contest encourages founders-in-the-making to think beyond prototypes.

SheInnovates nurtures an entrepreneurial mindset among students.

The event supports idea validation and exploration of sustainable business models.

Participants deliver investor-style pitches, enhancing presentation and communication skills.

The initiative helps build confidence, leadership, and clarity, preparing students for their startup journey.

Rules

Each team may submit only one idea, and a student cannot be part of more than one team.

The startup idea must be novel, technology-driven and problem-focused.

The pitch must align with the objectives and focus areas of the SheInnovates event.

Each team will be given a total of 10 minutes (7 minutes for pitching + 3 minutes for Q&A).

Each team's PPT must contain a maximum of 15 slides and must be submitted through the provided Google Form at least 2 hours before the start of the event.

Teams must report 15 minutes early and maintain professional conduct throughout the event.
