Rules & Guidelines

Participation Rules

  • Participation can be individual or in teams (maximum 3 members per team)
  • Projects must be related to emerging technologies listed above
  • All submissions must be original work of the participants
  • Participants must present a working model, prototype, or PPT
  • Teams must bring their own laptops, hardware components, and extension boards

Presentation Guidelines

Each team will be given 10 minutes for demonstration and explanation, followed by Q&A by judges.

Participants must clearly explain:

  • Problem Statement
  • Technology Used
  • Implementation
  • Innovation & Impact
  • Future Scope

Participants must report at the venue 30 minutes prior to the start time for setup.
