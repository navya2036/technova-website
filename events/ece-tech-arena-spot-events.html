TECH ARENA - SPOT EVENTS

Circuit Word Scramble

Participants are given jumbled ECE-related words.

They must unscramble the words correctly within the time limit.

Correct answer earns point.

Memory Matrix

Show 10 ECE-related words to participants for 30s.

After hiding the words, ask them to recall & write as many as possible.

Correct word with no spelling gives one point.

Tech Pictionary

One participant draws a word on paper, not allowed to write letters or numbers.

Other teammates must guess the correct word within time limit.

Tech Riddles (with space)

Participants are given simple riddles related to tech.

They must guess the object/technical term.

Rapid Fire

Participants asked simple ECE-related questions quickly.

They must answer immediately without hesitation.

Guess the Component

Participants are shown blur pictures of electronic components.

They must identify & name each component correctly.

ECE Words Chain Game

First participant says a technical word.

Next participant must say a word starting with last letter of previous word within time limit

Who says more wins.
