3D Innovate Hackathon

Team Size: 3

Event Description

3D Innovate Hackathon is a high-intensity innovation challenge focused on 3D printing, topology optimization, and product design thinking. Participants will be given a real-world engineering problem and develop an innovative, lightweight, and sustainable solution using CAD modelling and additive manufacturing concepts. Teams will conceptualize, design, simulate, and present their optimized prototype within the given time frame.

Rules

1. Each team must consist of a minimum of 1 member and a maximum of 3 members (only women).

2. The design problem will be announced during the event.

3. The solution must be designed and fabricated within the 36-hour duration.

4. Teams must use only the materials provided by the college.

5. 3D printing must be used as part of the final solution.

6. Participants must strictly follow the instructions and schedule given by the organizers.

7. Any unfair means or plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.

8. Evaluation Criteria: Innovation | Creativity | Uniqueness
